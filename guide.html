User Guide for Multiple Choice Quiz

1. Read each question carefully and select the correct option.

2. Each question has only one correct answer.

3. Ensure you have answered all questions before submitting.

4. Click the "Submit Answers" button to check your score.

5. Your score will be displayed, and you can review your answers.

User Guide for Structured Quiz

1. Read each question carefully and provide detailed answers.

2. Each question may have multiple parts; ensure all are answered.

3. Your answers should include the required information or calculations.

4. Ensure you have answered all questions before submitting.

5. Click the "Submit Answers" button to check your score.

6. Your score will be displayed, and you can review your answers.
